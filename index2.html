Game introduction close

There are 6 kinds of basic cards and 7 kinds of function cards , summing to 64 cards.

Basic cards

DNA ligase：ligase target gene fragment

restriction enzyme：Cut the DNA molecule fro m different source to gain DNA fragment what DNA in vitro

vector：Nucleic acid molecules which maintain Or express them stably that can carry foreign DNA fragments or genes into the receptor cell

target gene： A structural gene which encoding target protein (enzyme)

engineering bacteria: Foreign DNA ca n be ingested and stably maintained cells

PCR：Amplification method Of cloning vector

Function cards

phage：to be chow mechanism ， it can be used to chow one card before being played

antibiotic：it can attack bacteria

resistant gene：it can defend antibiotic

high temperature：it can make the enzyme inactivation

nuclease： it can attack target gene or vector

ultraviolet：it can attack target gene or vector

transfer：it can transfer attack to other people

You can see more details in help(?)

Rules

1.Each player initially gets 6 CARDS, and expresses the basic cards(the same card only sets one card), with the remaining CARDS in the hand

2.Start playing CARDS from you in a clockwise order (no CARDS at first), with only one card drawing and one card playing for each round

3.If you draw a basic card that you did not have before, you should directly express the card and continue to draw until you cannot

4.If playing the function card, you can arbitrarily designate the attacking player, and the attacked player will use the function card for defense; If there is no defense card, but there is an attacked card in hand can be directly expressed, do not occupy the turn

5.Attacks can only be transferred 1 time at most

6.The player who finally completes a complete work chain (that is, collect all 6 basic CARDS, no order required) wins

OI

1.Attack mode: click the function card in your hand, and then click the selected attack object. Description: antibiotics -> bacteria, high temperature -> restriction enzyme、DNA ligase, nuclease、ultraviolet-> target gene、vector

2.Defense mode: this game adopts mandatory automatic defense, when attacked, if there is a defense card in the hand, it must play, with defense instructions: resistant gene -> antibiotic, high temperature -> nuclease, transfer -> antibiotic、high temperature、 nuclease、 ultraviolet

3.Function card:double-click to discard; Basic card: click to discard.

4.If you choose wrong object, you will skip the draw as a penalty.

5.Hover your mouse over the corresponding image for more than 3 seconds to see the larger image.

6.Victory condition: if you collect 6 basic cards in your turn, you win
